About

I’m a data-driven researcher with a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering (GPA 3.8/4.0) from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic). My research focuses on building interpretable AI for healthcare — especially Graph Neural Networks for modeling medication safety and predicting potential drug–drug interactions using MIMIC-IV.

My work bridges deep learning, clinical data modeling, and interpretability — transforming EHR data into actionable insights for physicians and researchers.
