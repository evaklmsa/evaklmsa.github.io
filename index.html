About

I’m a data-driven researcher with a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering (GPA 3.8/4.0) from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic). My research focuses on building interpretable AI for healthcare — particularly using Graph Neural Networks to model medication safety and predict potential drug–drug interactions in MIMIC-IV and real-world datasets.

My work bridges deep learning, clinical data modeling, and interpretability — aiming to turn complex EHR data into actionable insights for physicians and researchers.
