About

I’m a data-driven researcher passionate about building interpretable AI systems for healthcare. I hold a B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering (GPA 3.8/4.0) from Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic) and currently focus on applying Graph Neural Networks for medication recommendation and drug–drug interaction prediction using MIMIC-IV.

My expertise spans machine learning, deep learning, and data science — from database design and KPI dashboards to predictive modeling, clustering-based segmentation, and demand forecasting. Certified in Applied Data Science by MIT Professional Education, I bridge research and implementation to deliver scalable, explainable AI solutions.
